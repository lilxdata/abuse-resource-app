Resource App

This app was designed to help people who know someone who has sexually assaulted someone else. We want to direct you to resources to help in the process of recovering from hearing about this abuse and preventing further abuse.

Finding out someone you know has committed a sexual assault is a hard thing to go through, and often it is not clear what you should do in the situation. Many people decide to either ignore the act that the person did or cut off contact with the person who has committed the sexual assault. While these are valid responses, neither of these reactions will prevent the person from committing sexual assaults in the future. It is good that you are here to help direct the person to resources to prevent future offenses, and to take care of yourself in the process.

We want to address all parts of the cycle of abuse in order to more quickly bring an end to it, and to stress that the burden is not on the person who has survived sexual assault to end sexual assault. Currently, this app does not have resources specifically for people who have survived sexual assault, so if that is you, visit RAINN to find resources for what to do after sexual assault.
