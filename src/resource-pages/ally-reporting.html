Reporting Resources

Reporting a sexual assault that someone you know committed can be hard for numerous reasons.

In order to report an assault, you can contact your local police department. However, if you do not feel comfortable engaging with the police, you can still keep the person who committed the assault accountable to their actions by using principles of community accountability. You can read more about that through Creative Intervention's guide for taking accountability.
